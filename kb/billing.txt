Billing Knowledge Base

--- Subscription ---
1. Our subscription plans are billed monthly or annually.
2. Users can upgrade or downgrade their subscription anytime.
3. Subscription changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.
4. Payment reminders are sent 3 days before renewal.
5. Invoices are available in the user dashboard.
6. Failed payments result in a 7-day grace period.
7. After the grace period, access is suspended until payment is received.
8. Users can cancel subscriptions from their account settings.
9. Cancellation stops future billing but does not refund past payments.
10. Subscription support is available via chat and email.

--- Refund Policy ---
11. Refunds are processed within 5 business days of approval.
12. Only payments made in the last 30 days are eligible for refund.
13. Refund requests must include a valid reason.
14. Partial refunds are available for annual plans.
15. Refunds are credited to the original payment method.

--- Payment Issues ---
16. For payment failures, check your card details and balance.
17. Contact support for unresolved payment issues.
18. We accept Visa, MasterCard, and PayPal.
19. Payment disputes are handled within 10 business days.
20. Payment history is accessible in your account.
