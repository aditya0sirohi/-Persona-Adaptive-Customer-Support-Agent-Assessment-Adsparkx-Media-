Analytics Knowledge Base

--- Dashboard ---
1. The dashboard provides an overview of key metrics.
2. Customize dashboard widgets to suit your needs.
3. Data refreshes every 15 minutes.
4. Export dashboard data to CSV or PDF.
5. Dashboard access is role-based.
6. Historical data is available for up to 2 years.
7. Dashboard themes can be changed in settings.
8. Alerts can be set for specific metrics.
9. Dashboard supports mobile and desktop views.
10. Share dashboard reports with your team.

--- Campaign Tracking ---
11. Track campaign performance in real time.
12. UTM parameters are supported for tracking sources.
13. Campaign analytics include click-through and conversion rates.
14. Set campaign goals and monitor progress.
15. Automated reports are sent weekly.

--- Reporting ---
16. Generate custom reports from analytics data.
17. Schedule reports for automatic delivery.
18. Reports can be filtered by date, campaign, or user.
19. Visualize data with charts and graphs.
20. Export reports to Excel, PDF, or Google Sheets.
